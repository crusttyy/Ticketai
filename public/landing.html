Frequently asked questions

Does TicketPilot auto-send replies?

Yes. TicketPilot can auto-send low-risk replies and route sensitive or low-confidence tickets to human review.

How does it stay on brand and policy?

Replies are grounded in your approved help content and policy documents, with configurable tone and response rules.

What happens when confidence is low?

TicketPilot routes those cases to your team with context and recommended next steps instead of forcing an uncertain response.

Which helpdesks are supported?

TicketPilot supports Zendesk, Intercom, and Freshdesk, with Salesforce Service Cloud available for enterprise plans.
